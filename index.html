Our Vision

The Place Hua Hin will be more than just a skatepark—it will be a vibrant community hub where creativity, culture, and sport collide. We're building a space that celebrates the raw energy of skateboarding while fostering connections between locals and visitors, artists and athletes, dreamers and doers.

Project Plan

Our vision spans across 2,500 square meters of prime Hua Hin real estate. The project will be developed in three phases over 18 months, starting with the core skatepark infrastructure, followed by community facilities, and finally the cultural programming spaces. We're targeting completion by Q4 2024, creating a year-round destination for both locals and international visitors.
