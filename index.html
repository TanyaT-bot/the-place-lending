Our Vision

THE PLACE is designed by riders and built to international standards, offering a space that feels pro while staying true to the street. It’s where skaters, BMX riders, artists, and food lovers come together to grow, learn, and connect — regardless of background.

But this is about more than sport. It’s about community, creativity, and cultural momentum. From youth programs to food, art, and music events — THE PLACE becomes the heartbeat of a new generation.

Our vision is to create a world-class action sports facility that serves as a hub for Thailand’s skateboarding, biking, and street culture scene — a place where passion meets purpose, and where global energy meets local roots.
